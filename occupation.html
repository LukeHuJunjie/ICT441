Employment direction

  • Participate in campus recruitment.
  • Participate in the civil service and public institution recruitment examination.
  • Participate in social recruitment, including online recruitment websites and offline job fairs.
  • Relatives, friends, teachers, etc., recommend jobs.
  • Be self-employed.
  • Cultivate confidence and enhance initiative
  • Provide targeted employment psychological guidance
